pizza



This pizza crust recipe was given to me by a friend. It is quick, easy, and delicious!
    Ingredients:
  • 500g water
  • 750g flour (use high-gluten bread flour)
  • 20g salt
  • 40g lard (at ROOM TEMPERATURE, DO NOT MELT IT!)
  • 10g active dry yeast OR 24g baker's (cake) yeast

    Prepare the Dough:
  1. Mix flour, yeast, water, olive oil, and salt to make a dough. Knead until smooth, then let it rise for about 1-2 hours in a warm place.
    2. Shape the Dough:
  2. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out or stretch it to your desired pizza shape on a floured surface.
    3. Add Toppings:
  3. Spread pizza sauce on the dough, then add your choice of toppings such as cheese, vegetables, meats, and seasonings.
    4. Bake:
  4. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Bake the pizza on a pizza stone or baking sheet for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
